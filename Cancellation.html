Order Cancellations

How to Cancel an Order

If you wish to cancel an order, you must visit the website where you completed the purchase and follow their cancellation process.

Most e-commerce platforms allow order cancellations through their order management section within a specific time period.

Cancellation rules, timelines, and eligibility depend on the policies of the respective e-commerce platform such as Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, or other partner websites.
